Passing conditions
  • attendance: 30%
  • homework: 10%
  • midterm examination: 30%
  • final examination: 30%
  • bonus for attendance: 2%
  • bonus for homework: 2%

Two unwarranted absences do not affect the final score. Each subsequent absence results in the attendance score being reduced by 10%, i.e. from 30% to 20%, from 20% to 10%, and from 10% to 0%. In order to pass the course, the student must attend at least 9 lectures and take the midterm and final examinations.

Grading scale
  • 93-100%: very good (5)
  • 85-92%: good plus (4.5)
  • 77-84%: good (4)
  • 69-76%: satisfactory plus (3.5)
  • 61-68%: satisfactory (3)
  • 0-60%: unsatisfactory (2)
Homework
  1. Do the homework given after each class.
  2. Upload a scanned copy to MS Teams no later than midnight between Friday and Saturday on the same week.
