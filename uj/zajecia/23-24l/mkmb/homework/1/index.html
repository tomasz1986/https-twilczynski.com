Dominika Lunitz - interview with Lee Joon-ik - “OTT로 돌아온 이준익 “불멸이 행복한 거냐, 자신에게 물었다”

번역

0-1. “OTT로 돌아온 이준익 “불멸이 행복한 거냐, 자신에게 물었다”

Return through OTT, Lee Joon-ik: “Is immortality happiness? I asked myself”

0-2. “불멸은 인간의 이기심이 만들어낸 꿈. 죽음과 디지털의 만남이 과연 행복한 것인지 묻게 된다.”

“Immortality is a dream created by human selfishness. It makes us question if the meeting of death and digital is really happiness.”

Lee Joon-ik the director of movies such as Dongju: The Portrait of a Poet and The Book of Fish is back with a futuristic sci-fi movie about human death and memory called Yonder.

As man who lost his wife, Jaehyun (Shin Ha-kyun), enters the “Yonder” repository which contains memories of his late wife Leehoo (Han Ji-min)

is a production that tells a story of a man who crosses the boundary of life and death.

The story takes place in 2032.

It is a future where euthanasia is legal

and the somewhat conceptual theme of “death” is the main subject of the work.

어휘

번역

“Yonder” is the first film in which director Lee Joon-ik meets the public through an online video service platform rather than cinema.

5. 글로벌 공개도 곧 앞두고 있는 이준익 감독을 지난 25일 서울 삼청동의 한 카페에서 만났다.

We have met the director Lee Joon-ik, who is ahead of a global release, at a cafe in Samcheong-dong, Seoul on the 25th of September.

  • 염두에 두다 - bare in mind

6. “과연 이 작품을 어떻게 봐 줄지, 대중의 반응에 대한 걱정은 있었다.

“Sure enough, I had worries how the public would react to this work”.

7. 글로벌 공개를 염두에 두고 만든 건 전혀 아니었지만, 안에서 새는 바가지가 밖에 나간다고 새지 않는 것도 아니고.(웃음)

It was not made with a global release in mind at all, but it's not like an already leaky bowl will stop leaking when taken outside. (laughs)

8-1. 망신만 당하지 말자는 심정으로 기다리는 중이다”라며

He said: “I am waiting only with wishes not to be embarrassed”,

8-2. 심경을 밝힌 그는 “영화라는 매체를 우리가 사실 서양에서 빌려 오긴 했지만

speaking his mind: “Although we borrowed the medium called film from the West,

8-3. 그릇에 담긴 내용물은 온전히 우리 거니까, 에스에프라는 장르를 바라보는 개인차, 취향차를 고려하면서, 배운다는 심정으로 만들었다”고 말했다.

the contents are completely ours, so we made [the movie] with a feeling of learning while considering individual differences and tastes regarding the SF genre.”

번역

9. 앞서 소개한 <욘더>의 주인공 재현과 이후의 이름에서 눈치챘듯, 남자의 이름은 삶을 뜻하는 현재를 가리키고 여자의 이름은 죽음 이후를 뜻한다.

You might have noticed that the previously introduced Yonder’s main characters' names Jaehyun and Leehoo refer to present (man’s name) and the afterlife (woman’s name).

10-1. “의도적으로 그렇게 이름을 지었다”는 이준익 감독은 11년 전 원작 소설 <굿바이, 욘더>(김장환 작가의 판타지소설)를 읽고

“I deliberately named them that way”, said director Lee Joon-ik, who 11 years ago read the original novel Goodbye, Yonder (a fantasy novel by Kim Jae-hwan) and

10-2. 난 뒤로 꾸준하게 삶과 죽음, 유한함과 불멸의 속성에 대한 자신의 생각을 다져 나갔다고 한다.

since then has been consistently refining his ideas about life, death, finiteness and immortality.

11. “시나리오를 쓰면서 불멸이 과연 행복한 것인지 스스로에게 묻게 됐다.

“Writing the screenplay made me ask myself if immortality is really happiness.

12. 인간의 이기심이 불멸을 꿈꾸게 하고, 그것이 곧 불행으로 이어진다.

Human selfishness leads to the dream of immortality, which ultimately leads to misery.

13. 이를 끝낼 유일한 방법은 유한성에 기대는 것이다.

The only way to end this is to lean on finitude.

14. <욘더>에서는 이런 저의 생각을 담아 봤다.”

Yonder reflects my thoughts on this.”

In Yonder a setting appears, which is reminiscent of the huge metaverse, in which virtual reality that we are familiar with and individual virtual reality meet together.

“I tried not to differentiate between the reality and metaverse, which belong to the world of Yonder.

To show its actual image, I made the set not different from reality”, he said, emphasizing that the sci-fi focuses on portraying the characters’ emotions.
