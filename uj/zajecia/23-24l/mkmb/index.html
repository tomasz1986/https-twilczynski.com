Homework

Homework 1

  1. After each class, finish translating the text.
  2. Firstly, after each Korean sentence, write its English translation.
  3. Secondly, fix the translation, so that the whole paragraph sounds naturally.
  4. Send the finished translation to tomasz1.wilczynski@uj.edu.pl by midnight on Sunday in the same week.

Homework 2

  1. Choose a subject, which you are interested in, and then propose it during the class on March 7.
  2. By e-mail, you will receive a link to a newspaper article on the selected topic.
  3. Divide the article into 3-4 sentence paragraphs, and then, if possible, divide each sentence into shorter fragments as long as each of them makes sense.
  4. Before each paragraph, prepare a list of new vocabulary and grammatical structures.
  5. Translate the whole text in the same manner as texts translated during the class.
  6. Send the finished translation to tomasz1.wilczynski@uj.edu.pl by midnight on Friday in a week that precedes your homework presentation date.
  7. Guide translation of a selected text fragment together with the group on a previously set date.
